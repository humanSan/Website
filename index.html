Projects

EHR Deidentify

EHR Deidentify is an AI powered health data deidentification app that removs personal information from electronic health records. It can also generate a password-encrypted reidentification map for reidentification by authorized personnel later.

Features

  • Removes personal health information from a health record based on the user's selection of which information types to remove (name, dates, addresses, etc.) to create a new deidentified record with placeholder values
  • Allows the user to create a password and creates a reidentification map encrypted using secure SHA-256 algorithm.
  • Previously deidentified records can be restored to their original form including personal information after entering reidentification map and correct password.

ReviewerLLM

ReviewerLLM is an AI powered product review assistant that searches through online product reviews and analyzes them to discover insights on product features, strengths, and weaknesses. ReviewerLLM is an essential tool for online shoppers, making it easy for them to make product purchase decision based on what they are looking for and what previous users of products have said. It's also vital for product teams seeking to understand user concerns to fix issues or improve future products.

Features

  • Generates a summarized report of the most important reviews addressing strengths, weaknesses, strategies for product improvement, etc.
  • Answered direct questions from the user using relevant reviews about a product as reference.
  • Ussd sentiment analysis assisted retrieval augmented generation (RAG) for accurate context retrieval and the latest Gemini model for robust responses.
